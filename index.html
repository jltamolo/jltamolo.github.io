About me

My name is Juliet Onyango, an IT specialist based in Nairobi, Kenya. I'm passionate about using technology to create useful solutions. I bring a solid foundation to the ever-evolving field of information technology, having earned a Bachelors degree in Information Technology.


My path has included a wide range of experiences, from working directly on software development projects to offering strategic IT consulting services. I have a sharp eye for streamlining IT procedures and thrive on the challenge of addressing difficult challenges.


While based in Nairobi, I am excited about embracing remote opportunities and engaging with varied teams from any corner of the globe.
