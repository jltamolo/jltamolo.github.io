Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me and my development process.

How long does a typical project take? +

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website typically takes 1-3 weeks, custom web applications take 6-12 weeks and complex e-commerce solutions can take 12-16 weeks. I provide detailed timelines during project planning.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance? +

Yes! All my projects include ongoing support ranging from 3-12 months depending on the price package. This includes bug fixes, security updates, performance optimization and technical support.

Can you work with existing systems? +

Absolutely. I can audit, redesign and enhance existing platforms for better performance, scalability and design.
